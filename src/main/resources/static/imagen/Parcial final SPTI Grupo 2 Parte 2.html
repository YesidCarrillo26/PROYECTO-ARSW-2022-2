  • General

    General

    Welcome everybody to the most exiting course about Cybersecurity you would ever take!

    The information and its security are issues of great importance and increasing relevance in all organizations. The country and the world need to have professionals trained in the area of security of information and particularly, cybersecurity due it has great relevance because of all the digital assets that are stored and processed daily. In this course we will go delve into the most important aspects of cybersecurity. 

    In this space we will be publishing the material of the class (presentations, readings, assignments, tasks, etc).

    Remember: This is a course aimed to learn what is cybersecurity and its implications, giving in this way a strong technical background needed for the protection of information assets (information, network, software, hardware and people) in a corporate environment. Hence, all the topics that are taught in classes and laboratories are seen from the perspective of the security manager (CISO) or the cybersecurity practitioners.

  • AAA

    AAA

    An important component of cybersecurity refers to the AAA (Authentication, Authorization, Availability) framework. AAA guarantees i) Authentication: The users are who they say they are,  ii) Authorization: Verify that users can perform the actions that they are authorized to, and iii) Availability: Validate that the actions that are carried out in the systems have a trace.
